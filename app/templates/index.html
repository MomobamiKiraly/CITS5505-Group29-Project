About F1 Friends
  • Step 1 – Register: Create your account and select your favorite F1 team and driver.
  • Step 2 – Upload: Submit your race predictions via the Upload tab.
  • Step 3 – Analyze: View automated visualizations of current standings and your predictions.
  • Step 4 – Share: Follow friends and choose to share your prediction data with them.
  • Step 5 – Chat: Privately chat with mutual followers and discuss predictions together.

Your data is kept private by default. You choose what to share and with whom.

Built with Flask, Bootstrap, SQLite and Chart.js — designed for F1 fans and data lovers.
